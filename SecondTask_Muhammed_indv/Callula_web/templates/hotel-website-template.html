Welcome to Cellula Hotel: Where Statistics Meet Luxury!

Greetings, data enthusiasts and comfort seekers! Welcome to Cellula Hotel, where we take your stay as seriously as a statistician takes their p-values. Did you know our average guest spends precisely 2.7 nights with us during the week? That's right, we're so comfortable, people forget to check out! And weekends? Well, let's just say our "Distribution of Weekend Nights" graph looks suspiciously like our guests' heart rates when they realize they have to leave. Now, about children - our "Number of Children" distribution shows a mean of 0.11. We're not entirely sure what 0.11 of a child looks like, but we promise to accommodate all 0.11 of them with the utmost care! Speaking of care, our "lead time" graph suggests that some of you plan your stays faster than you can say "spontaneous vacation," while others meticulously plot their visits like it's a mission to Mars. Don't worry, we love you all equally - just don't ask us about our bell curve of guest quirkiness! So whether you're here for a week, a weekend, or just long enough to skew our data, make yourself at home. At Cellula Hotel, we're not just a place to stay; we're a statistically significant experience!.
